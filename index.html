Help Us Find Loving Homes for Cats

Our mission is to help cats find their forever homes. Cats are wonderful companions with unique personalities and lots of love to give. Join us in our efforts to ensure every cat has a loving family to call their own. From adoption resources to helpful tips on cat care, we provide everything you need to know to make a difference in a cat’s life.

Curiosities About Adoption

  • Adopting a cat from a shelter can save two lives: the cat you adopt and the cat that takes its place in the shelter.
  • Many shelter cats are already trained and have known temperaments, making it easier to find a cat that fits well with your lifestyle.
  • Adopting a cat often comes with initial veterinary care, such as vaccinations and spaying/neutering, reducing the initial costs for new pet owners.
  • Black cats are often overlooked in shelters due to superstitions and myths. Adopting a black cat can save a life and break these misconceptions.
  • Senior cats are less likely to be adopted but can make wonderful companions. They are often more mellow and already have established personalities.

Understanding these curiosities can help more people choose adoption and provide loving homes for cats in need.

Top 3 Reasons to Adopt a Cat

Here are the top three reasons to consider adopting a cat:

Save a Life

By adopting a cat, you are giving a homeless animal a second chance at life. Shelters are often overcrowded, and adopting helps reduce the number of cats in need of homes.

Find a Loyal Companion

Adopted cats often show immense gratitude and form strong bonds with their new families. They can be incredibly loyal and loving companions.

Support Ethical Pet Ownership

Adopting a cat supports ethical pet ownership by reducing the demand for breeding mills, which often have poor conditions. It also helps combat the issue of pet overpopulation.

Ways to Get a Cat

Here are some ways you can help cats find their forever homes:

Find a Street Cat

Street cats often need a loving home. You can rescue and provide care for a stray cat that you find in your neighborhood.

Adoption

Adopting a cat from a shelter or rescue organization is a wonderful way to give a cat a second chance at a happy life. Shelters often have cats of all ages and personalities.

Foster a Cat

Fostering a cat provides a temporary home until they find their forever family. It's a great way to help cats in need while enjoying the companionship of a cat.
